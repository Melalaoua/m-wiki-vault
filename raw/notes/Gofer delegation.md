There a basically two kinds of delegation '[[Gofer delegation]]' and '[[Stewardship delegation]]'. gofer delegations means "Go for this, go for that, do this, do that, and tell me when it's done".

Most people who are producers have a gofer delegation paradigm. Remember the machete wielders in the jungle ? They are the producers. They roll up their sleeves and get the job done. If they are given a position of supervision or management, they still think like producers.

There's much better way, a more effective way to delegate to other people. And it's based on a paradigm of appreciation of the self-awareness, the imagination, the conscience, and the free will of other people.
