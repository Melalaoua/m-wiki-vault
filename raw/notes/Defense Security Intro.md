Blue teams are part of the defense security, 1. they aim to prevent intrusions from ocurring 2. They detect intrusions when they occur and respond accordingly


### SOC : Security Operations Center
Team of cyber security monitoring the network and its sytems to detect attacks and vulnerabilities. 
- When a vulnerabilities is discovered ; respond accordingly with a quick and efficient patch/update.
- Identify policy violations by users of the protected network.
- Detect any unwanted/unauthorized activities.
- Detect as soon as possible network intrusions.

Gather intelligence about the attackers, to achieve *threat-informed defence*. Different companies have different adversaries (politicial, financial, direct concurrent, ..). Gathering information about the attacker can help identify their target and their way of operating.

### DFIR : Digital Forensic and Incident Response.

#### Digital Forensic
Using science to investigate crimes. The focus of digital forensic includes low-level copy of the filesystem and memory of the attacker, analysis of system logs and network logs. Any trace left by the attacker can help the case.

#### Incident Response
When a data breach of cyber attack or lesser events (misconfiguration, intrusion attempt, ...) occurs ; a correct response must be organized. Incident response specifies the methodology that should be followed to handle such a case, reducing damage and recovering in the shortest amount of time.

1. Preparation : Having a team prepared and ready to handle such events.
2. Detection and analysis : detect any incident and be enough informed about the severity, origin of attack.
3. Containment, Eradication, Recovery : stop the breach, eliminate it and recover the affected systems. 
4. Post-incident Activity : lesson learned, won't happen again.
