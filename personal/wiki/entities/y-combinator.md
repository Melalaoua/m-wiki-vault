# Y Combinator

[[y-combinator|Y Combinator]] is an organization functioning as a hybrid between an investment firm and an educational program for startup founders. Co-founded in 2005 by [[paul-graham|Paul Graham]] and [[wiki/entities/jessica-livingston|Jessica Livingston]], it is renowned for cultivating high-growth startups and focusing heavily on metrics like monthly [[wiki/concepts/startup-growth-rate|growth rate]] and deep user empathy. By 2026, the firm had funded approximately 6,500 companies.
