# Ken Muneoka

Dr. Ken Muneoka is a professor at the [[wiki/entities/texas-a-and-m-university|Texas A&M College of Veterinary Medicine and Biomedical Sciences]] (VMBS). His research focuses on solving the limits of mammalian [[wiki/concepts/regeneration]]. His work has successfully redirected [[wiki/concepts/fibroblast]] behavior in mammals to form [[wiki/concepts/blastema]]-like structures instead of scars using a combination of [[wiki/concepts/fibroblast-growth-factor-2|FGF2]] and [[wiki/concepts/bone-morphogenetic-protein-2|BMP2]].
