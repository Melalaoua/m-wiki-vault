# Deliberate Practice in Gaming

[[Deliberate Practice in Gaming]] is the shift from high-volume, automated play to highly intentional, focused learning loops.\n\n### Key Principles\n- **De-automation**: Comfort and automation are the enemies of skill acquisition. Automated mistakes are still mistakes; they are simply comfortable ones.\n- **Intention Over Volume**: One fully present, intensely focused game that is reviewed honestly is worth more than ten mindless matches played on muscle memory.\n- **Theory over Concepts**: Focus on acquiring holistic principles (the 'why') rather than copying situational shortcuts (the 'what'). Copying pro players without their foundational reasoning fails when context changes.\n- **Unlocking Aggression**: Passivity is a habit of hesitation. Players should practice playing aggressively to find, hit, and learn their technical boundaries.
