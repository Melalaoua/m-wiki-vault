# Permutation Invariance

In machine learning, [[Permutation Invariance]] refers to a model's ability to produce the same output regardless of the order of its inputs. This is a critical inductive bias for [[graph-neural-network]] models, as the specific ordering of an adjacency matrix or adjacency list should not change the predictions made about the graph.
