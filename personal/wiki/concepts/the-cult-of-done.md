# The Cult of Done

A 13-point manifesto written by [[bre-pettis]] and [[kio-stark]] in 2009. It champions rapid iteration, discarding perfectionism, and treating completion as a stepping stone rather than a final state. Key tenets include viewing everything as a draft, accepting failure as a form of completion, and combating [[Per-Project Procrastination]] by dropping stagnant ideas.
