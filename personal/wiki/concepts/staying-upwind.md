# Staying Upwind

[[staying-upwind]] is a heuristic for career and project planning. Rather than creating a rigid, long-term master plan (which only works for highly predictable achievements like winning a medal), you optimize your path by making choices at each stage that seem the most interesting and give you the best options for the future. This approach maximizes serendipity and [[wiki/concepts/exponential-growth]].
