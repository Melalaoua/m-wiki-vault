# The 3% Approach

The 3% percent approach is a creative philosophy attributed to [[virgil-abloh]]. It suggests taking an existing, proven design and altering only 3% of it. This method acts as a forcing function to deeply understand the original work before making changes. In practice, honest iteration from this baseline naturally drifts beyond 3%, often reaching 50% or more as specific brand and contextual needs are met.
