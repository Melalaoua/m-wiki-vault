# FIRE (Financial Independence, Retire Early)

A personal finance movement emphasizing aggressive savings, debt elimination, and strategic investments to achieve financial independence. Key metrics include the savings rate and the 'FI Number', which is typically calculated based on a safe withdrawal rate against rolling annual expenses. Progress is heavily dependent on maintaining comprehensive, automated tracking, often facilitated by tools like [[financial-maturity-via-talis|Talis]].
