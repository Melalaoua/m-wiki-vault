# Vibe Medicine

Vibe Medicine is an AI paradigm where medical decision-support systems continuously evolve by learning from real-world clinical interactions and accumulating experience. Inspired by "vibe coding" and "vibe research," it represents a shift from static, pre-trained [[wiki/concepts/large-language-models]] toward dynamic, [[wiki/concepts/experience-driven-ai]] agents that autonomously refine their decision-making protocols and behaviors over time through reflection and implicit feedback.
