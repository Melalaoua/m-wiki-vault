# Embedding Space

An **embedding space** is a vector space where categorical or complex data (such as words, users, or items) are represented as dense vectors to capture their relationships and semantic meanings. By projecting data into this space, complex relationships are translated into geometric distances and angles, enabling algebraic comparisons. This is a foundational concept in [[Natural Language Processing]] (utilizing models like [[Word2Vec]] and [[BERT]]) and in modern [[Recommendation System]]s.
