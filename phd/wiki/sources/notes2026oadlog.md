# OAD(log) - Beta-Bangui

Original: [[raw/notes/OAD(log) - Beta-Bangui]]

Target date: 21st June.

Tablets will be equipped with the [[phd/wiki/maps/oad-mobile-health-application|OAD Mobile Health Application]] and handed off to Bangui in Africa for internal testing and return. 

Version 1.2.0 will be built based on this feedback for a final public launch in September.

## Key claims

- Internal testing in Bangui for the tablet application begins on June 21st. ([[raw/notes/OAD(log) - Beta-Bangui#Main text]]) — "Target date : 21th June. Tablets will be equipped with application and handoff to Bangui in Africa for internal testing and return."
- Version 1.2.0 will be launched publicly in September following internal feedback. ([[raw/notes/OAD(log) - Beta-Bangui#Main text]]) — "1.2.0 will be build based and critics for September final launch in public."
