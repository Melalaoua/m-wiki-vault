# Andrej Karpathy

A prominent researcher in [[phd/wiki/concepts/machine-learning|Machine Learning]] and [[phd/wiki/concepts/computer-vision|Computer Vision]]. He is the author of the influential blog post "A Survival Guide to a PhD", which details strategies for research, academic writing, and navigating the [[phd/wiki/maps/phd|PhD]] journey.
