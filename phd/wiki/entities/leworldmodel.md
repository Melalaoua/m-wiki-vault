# LeWorldModel

An end-to-end [[Joint-Embedding Predictive Architecture]] developed by Lucas Maes, Quentin Le Lidec, et al. It learns a compact latent world model from pixels using a two-term loss, significantly speeding up planning compared to approaches like [[DINO-WM]] while effectively learning physical scene dynamics.
