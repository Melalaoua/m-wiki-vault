# Thesis Topic Shift: World Models vs LLMs

## Goal

Evaluate limits and strengths of LLMs vs World Models in clinical and molecular diagnosis to prepare a thesis topic shift pitch for the supervisor in 2-3 weeks.

## Open questions

- What are the limits and strengths of LLMs in clinical & molecular diagnosis?
- What are the strengths of World Models and how can they be translated to genomic/clinical fields?
- Is the novelty of World Models worth the risk of their unproven real-world reliability compared to LLMs?
