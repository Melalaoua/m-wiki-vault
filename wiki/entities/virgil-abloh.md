# Virgil Abloh

[[wiki/entities/virgil-abloh]] was a highly influential creative and designer. He is frequently cited for coining the [[wiki/concepts/3-percent-approach]], a philosophy he notably applied when designing his series of Air Force 1s. This method involved mimicking an existing, distinguished design while committing to modifying just 3% to avoid diluting the original vision.
