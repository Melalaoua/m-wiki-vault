# Great Work

[[wiki/concepts/great-work]] is the output of working ambitiously at the intersection of natural aptitude and deep interest. According to [[wiki/entities/paul-graham]], doing great work involves a four-step process:
1. Choosing a field.
2. Reaching the frontier of knowledge in that field.
3. Noticing anomalies and gaps.
4. Exploring those gaps bravely.

It requires avoiding affectation, cultivating [[wiki/concepts/curiosity]], managing [[wiki/concepts/morale]], and iterating constantly rather than strictly planning.

Source: [[wiki/sources/paulgraham2026great|Great Work]]
