# Organic Startup Idea Generation

According to investors like [[wiki/entities/paul-graham|Paul Graham]], the most effective [[wiki/concepts/startup-idea-generation|method for finding startup ideas]] is to not look for them intentionally. Actively hunting for business concepts typically results in overly conservative ideas that miss outliers. Instead, the best startups often begin as informal side projects built with friends to solve a personal need. Because young founders' needs often predict future mass-market demand, optimizing for what you and your peers find cool frequently yields the strongest foundation for high-growth companies.
