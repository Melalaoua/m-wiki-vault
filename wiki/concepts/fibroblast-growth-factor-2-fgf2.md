# Fibroblast Growth Factor 2 (FGF2)

Fibroblast growth factor 2 (FGF2) is a growth factor involved in wound healing and tissue development. In the field of [[wiki/maps/regenerative-medicine]], it has been demonstrated to induce [[wiki/concepts/blastema]]-like formations in mammals, effectively shifting the cellular response of fibroblasts away from [[wiki/concepts/fibrosis]] when applied to healed wounds.
