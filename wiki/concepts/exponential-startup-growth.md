# Exponential Startup Growth

[[wiki/concepts/exponential-growth|Exponential growth]] is the underlying mathematical mechanic that allows startup founders to reach billion-dollar net worths rapidly. The success formula relies heavily on two variables: the [[wiki/concepts/startup-growth-rate|growth rate]] (driven by creating a product users love enough to share) and the duration of growth, which is dictated by the [[wiki/concepts/market-size|size of the market]]. For instance, a startup compounding at 15% monthly growth will scale its revenue by over 4,000 times in just five years.
