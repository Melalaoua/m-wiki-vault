# COL1-Related Connective Tissue Disorders

[[wiki/concepts/type-i-collagen|Type I collagen]], encoded by the [[wiki/entities/col1a1|COL1A1]] and [[wiki/entities/col1a2|COL1A2]] genes, is fundamental to connective tissue health. Variants in these genes can lead to a spectrum of disorders with overlapping clinical features.

*   **[[wiki/concepts/osteogenesis-imperfecta|Osteogenesis Imperfecta (OI)]]**: Primarily characterized by bone fragility, but can also involve cardiovascular abnormalities.
*   **[[wiki/concepts/ehlers-danlos-syndrome|Ehlers-Danlos Syndrome (EDS)]]**: A group of disorders featuring joint hypermobility, skin hyperextensibility, and tissue fragility. Certain subtypes are directly linked to COL1 variants.
*   **[[wiki/concepts/early-onset-bicuspid-aortic-valve-disease|Early-Onset Bicuspid Aortic Valve Disease (EBAV)]]**: While [[wiki/concepts/bicuspid-aortic-valve|BAV]] is a congenital heart defect, rare COL1 variants act as modifiers that accelerate complications, creating a phenotype that can overlap with mild features of OI and EDS.
*   **COL1-Related Overlap Disorder (C1ROD)**: A recognized condition with mixed features of both OI and EDS.
